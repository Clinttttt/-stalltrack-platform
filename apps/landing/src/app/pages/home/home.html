The problem

Manual operations slow down revenue collection.

Many Economic Enterprise Management Offices and public market / facility operations still depend on manual records, paper-based collections, scattered reports, and difficult audit trails.

Paper-based collections

Daily and monthly fees logged on paper are slow to total and easy to misplace.

Scattered records

Stall, tenant, and contract details live across notebooks, spreadsheets, and folders.

Hard audit trails

Tracing who collected what, when, and under which OR number is tedious and error-prone.

Manual reporting

Monthly and yearly reports are re-compiled by hand, delaying decisions and reconciliation.

The solution

Everything in one digital platform.

StallTrack centralizes stall records, rental collections, payment tracking, reports, audit logs, and dashboards — so offices spend less time on paperwork and more on service.

  • One source of truth for stalls, tenants, and contracts
  • Faster, traceable rental and fee collections
  • Reports and dashboards generated automatically
  • A complete audit trail on every transaction

Facility types

One platform, many facility types.

StallTrack models facilities as configurable types — each with its own billing rhythm. Cantilan's EEMO, our first deployment, runs the eight below; the names, rates, and rules adapt to any LGU's enterprises.

Not locked to one LGU. Facility names and fee structures are configured per office — billing models (daily, monthly, per-head, per-trip, per-vendor) are reused across markets, terminals, slaughterhouses, and more.

Daily

Public Market

Per-stall daily fees with optional utilities and per-kilo produce or fish charges.

e.g. New Public Market (NPM)
Monthly

Commercial Center

Fixed monthly rent for commercial stalls and units, with contract terms and expiry.

e.g. Tampak / New Commercial Center
Monthly

Food & BBQ Stalls

Monthly space rental for food courts and barbecue vendors.

e.g. Barbecue Stand (BBQ)
Monthly

Iceplant

Monthly space and utility rental for cold-storage or ice production.

e.g. Iceplant (ICE)
Per head

Slaughterhouse

Per-animal fees by type (hog, cattle, carabao) with official-receipt entry.

e.g. Slaughterhouse (SLH)
Per trip

Transport Terminal

Per-trip dispatch fees with queuing and departure-order tracking.

e.g. Transport Terminal (TRM)
Per vendor

Weekly / Tabo-an Market

Per-vendor fees collected on scheduled market days (e.g. every Friday).

e.g. Tabo-an Public Market (TPM)

Security & privacy

Private by design.

StallTrack treats operational data as private. This website is a public company page — the admin system itself is access-controlled and does not need to be public.

Role-based access

Head, Admin, and Collector roles with least-privilege, facility-scoped permissions.

Admin-only dashboard

The operational system stays private behind authentication — not publicly exposed.

Audit trail support

Financial transactions are recorded with actor, timestamp, and before/after values.

Secure records management

Operational data is handled privately and never surfaced on this public site.
